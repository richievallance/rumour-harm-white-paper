# Front Cover — Rumour Harm and Institutional Communication
## Element: Air / Social Transmission / Digital Contagion

### Image Generation Prompt

A serious sociology policy-paper cover. Background: clean institutional cream or pale grey. Central visual: an abstract network diagram showing invisible transmission lines between silhouetted figures and institutional building outlines — the lines of rumour spreading, fragmenting, and reconstituting. Overlaid: fragments of formal documents (unreadable but recognisably official — letters, records). The network lines are precise, geometric, cool — social science, not emotion. Bottom: a horizontal band in muted civic blue with title. Serious, restrained, suitable for a public policy audience. Civic blue, institutional grey, cream. No Leonardo imagery. Modern academic white paper design. 4:6 portrait.
